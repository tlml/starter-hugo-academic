abstract: The variety of isotopes in cosmic rays allows us to study different aspects
  of the processes that cosmic rays undergo between the time they are produced and
  the time of their arrival in the heliosphere. In this paper, we present measurements
  of the isotopic ratios $^2$H/$^4$He, $^3$He/$^4$He, $^6$Li/$^7$Li, $^7$Be/($^9$Be+$^10$Be),
  and $^10$B/$^11$B in the range 0.2-1.4 GeV of kinetic energy per nucleon. The measurements
  are based on the data collected by the Alpha Magnetic Spectrometer, AMS-01, during
  the STS-91 flight in 1998 June.
